19.        The Sprint Session (events 101 – 108):

  • The idea is that the best sprinter will emerge after four swims

  • These items are only for competitors 13 years and over

  • The first session on Friday evening will be a preliminary swim-off to arrive at the fastest 16 times for each event

  • The second last round will be the semi-finals or best 8

  • The final: - The best 4 sprinters at the gala determine the Sprint Champion.

Points will be awarded on the following basis, but not for the 50m sprints in the 11/12 years and 13 & over.
