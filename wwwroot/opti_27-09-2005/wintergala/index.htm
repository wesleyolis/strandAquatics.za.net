 The Strand Winter Gala was started in 1992, firstly as an opportunity for swimmers to participate early in the season and secondly as a fundraiser for the Club.  The annual fundraiser is the only way to boost club funds to afford swimmers to compete in various national galas and to subsidise some of our developmental swimmers.

The Strand Winter gala is the first national club gala on Swim South Africa's calendar of the new swimming season, which starts in May.  This premier event attracts more than 600 swimmers from 20-30 Clubs from the Western Cape, Northern Cape, Eastern Cape, Gauteng and Namibia.
