Members of the Strand Aquatic Club undertake as follows:

1.         To generally accept the code of conduct of Swimming South Africa and the STRAND AQUATIC CLUB;

2.         To comply with the dress code established by the STRAND AQUATIC CLUB, including wearing the recognised clothing and kit for travelling, training and competition purposes;

3.         To neither take nor use drugs or stimulants prohibited by SWIM SOUTH AFRICA/FINA rules.

4.         To observe and comply with all decisions and orders given by the appointed Team Officials.

5.         To do your best to render yourself fit for training or galas as required.

6.         To maintain proper and functioning equipment and have it available for each practice.

7.         To not advocate or participate in any illegal activity or be obscene, abusive or otherwise harass any person whilst participating in activities associated with the STRAND AQUATIC CLUB.

8.         To show respect for other swimmers by not behaving in a manner that disrupts their performance, either at practices or at galas.

9.         To be on time for practices so as not to disrupt other swimmer’s training session.

10.       To remain in the control of the coach at all times.

11.      To notify the coach of any injury that might prejudice him/her from taking part in practices or galas.

12.       To show respect for the coach at all times.

13.       To conduct yourself in a proper manner in the pool, on the deck and on the stands so as not to bring yourself, the STRAND AQUATIC CLUB, the club management and your fellow team members into public disrepute or censure.

14.       In the event of any breach of the terms and conditions of any of the above by a swimmer the following will apply:
i.   a written complaint either by the coach or complainant must be issued within 5 days.
ii.  The Chairman together with the Chairperson of the Disciplinary Committee will issue the
     swimmer with a written notice when to appear before the Disciplinary committee.
